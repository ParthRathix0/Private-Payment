│ ❓ DO YOU NEED CHAIN PARAMETERS?                             │

NO! ✅

For dummy proofs, you DON'T need:
  ❌ Block numbers
  ❌ Timestamps
  ❌ Chain IDs
  ❌ Gas prices
  ❌ Nonces

The dummy verifier ignores the proof and returns true!

Only the PUBLIC SIGNALS matter:
  ✅ Current balances (checked by contract)
  ✅ New balance (what you want)
  ✅ Nullifier (for tracking)
